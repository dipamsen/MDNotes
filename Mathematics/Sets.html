Sets

A set is a well defined collection of objects.

The following are some examples of valid sets. N=\{1,2,3,4,5,\cdots\} V=\{A, E, I, O, U\} A=\text{set of all students in a class}

The following examples are not well defined, thus they cannot be called sets. \text{Eg: set of the most toughest chapters in JEE Mains}
